I · The Necessary Correction

All of his formulas are proven. That is not the point. 一个更深的开放性

Correction to earlier framing

An earlier version of this argument claimed Ramanujan left "unproven results." This is technically wrong and must be corrected. Bruce Berndt — the world's foremost authority on Ramanujan's notebooks — has confirmed: "As far as I am aware, there is no claim or conjecture that is still unresolved. There are only statements to which we have not yet been able to give meaning." Every individual identity has been proved. The real open territory is something deeper and stranger than a list of unverified formulas.

What Ramanujan left behind was not a debt of unverified claims. He left something more unsettling: an unfinished classification system. His 17 mock theta functions — described in his final letter to Hardy, weeks before his death at 32 — came with no definition, no unifying principle, no theory. He knew they formed a class. He could not say why.

Freeman Dyson named the wound precisely in 1987:

"The mock theta functions give us tantalizing hints of a grand synthesis still to be discovered… This remains a challenge for the future." — Freeman Dyson, 1987 Ramanujan Centenary Lecture

The grand synthesis — the underlying 理 that organises the whole class — is what is still missing. The Western tradition has supplied the proofs of individual identities by placing them inside existing frameworks (modular forms, harmonic Maass forms, q-series). But placing objects inside a framework is not the same as understanding why the class coheres from the inside.

This is exactly the distinction 算學 is built to make. And it is where the encounter becomes genuinely productive rather than merely analogical.

What is open — a corrected map
GENUINELY OPEN
The intrinsic definition of "order" for mock theta functions. "3rd order", "5th order" are labels, not a theorem. What structural property makes a function 3rd order rather than 5th?
FRAMEWORK INCOMPLETE
A Hecke theory for mock theta functions — the analogue of Hecke operators acting on ordinary modular forms — does not exist. The algebraic symmetry structure is unwritten.
PARTIALLY RESOLVED
Ramanujan–Petersson for Maass forms — the real-analytic counterpart to Deligne's holomorphic proof — remained open through 2025. Claimed proofs (Unterberger, 13 revisions) unaccepted.
NO BIJECTIVE PROOF EXISTS
The Rogers–Ramanujan identities are analytically proved but have no explicit bijection between the two partition sets. A bijection = a shù. None found.
COMBINATORIAL GAP
Partition congruences p(pn+a) ≡ 0 mod p are proved for all primes. But the explicit combinatorial witness (rank works for p=5,7; crank for p=11; nothing for p≥13).
II · The Deep Pattern

Two civilisations. One epistemic instinct. 一个认识论家族

Ramanujan learned mathematics from Carr's Synopsis of Pure Mathematics — a list of 5,000 results with almost no proofs. He absorbed it as pure pattern: the result is the knowledge. The proof, if it comes, is the record of having confirmed what was already perceived.

算學 would not have found this strange. In the Nine Chapters and Liu Hui's commentary, a method (shù 術) is valid when it reliably produces the correct output from any valid input. Legitimacy flows from operational invariance, not from derivation from prior truths. The proof is not the source of validity — it is the trace of a shù that has been run long enough to reveal its 理.

Both traditions share a single root intuition: mathematical reality is perceived before it is proved. What Ramanujan called receiving from Namagiri, 算學 calls the 理 emerging from the shù. Different vocabularies. Same epistemic structure: the seeing is prior; the proof is the trace of the seeing.

The key difference is that 算學 institutionalised this. It built a systematic discipline — algorithm catalogues, verification protocols, constructive proofs — around the instinct that Ramanujan was operating from in isolation, without support, inside a tradition that didn't share it.

Ramanujan was doing mathematics from inside a non-axiomatic epistemic tradition — one where legitimacy flows from structural coherence, not derivability. 算學 is the most fully developed historical instance of exactly such a tradition. III · Partitions as Board Configurations

p(n): counting rod arrangements 分拆即阵列

A partition of n is, in 算學 terms, a valid column configuration on the rod board — arrange columns of heights summing to n, in non-increasing order. p(n) counts how many such configurations exist for a given n.

Ramanujan's three congruences, spotted by pure inspection of tables he had computed by hand:

witness gap
p(5n + 4) ≡ 0 (mod 5) ← explained by Dyson rank (1944) p(7n + 5) ≡ 0 (mod 7) ← explained by Dyson rank (1944) p(11n + 6) ≡ 0 (mod 11) ← crank found Andrews-Garvan (1988) p(13n + a) ≡ 0 (mod 13) ← explicit witness: none known p(pn + a) ≡ 0 (mod p) ← general combinatorial structure: open

Dyson's "rank" (largest part minus number of parts) partitions the residue classes for mod 5 and mod 7. For mod 11, he predicted a statistic he named "the crank" — without being able to define it. The crank was found 44 years later.

The 算學 question is different from the Western one. Western mathematics asks: does a statistic exist? 算學 asks: what transformation on the board cycles through the p classes? Not a static label — an explicit move. A shù of order p on the partition lattice. For p=5 and p=7 the move is known. For p≥13 it has never been constructed.

Multiple proof paths illuminate the same congruence from different angles — in the 算學 tradition, this proof multiplicity is itself information. The invariant that survives all paths is the 理. The congruence is not merely true — it is multi-transformationally stable.

IV · Mock Theta Functions

The almost-closed array 近不变量阵列

In January 1920, three months before his death, Ramanujan wrote his final letter to Hardy. He described 17 new objects he called "mock theta functions" — provided examples, no definition, no theory. He wrote that they entered mathematics "in a manner exactly analogous to the ordinary theta functions" but were "totally different from what they look."

Sander Zwegers resolved the mystery in 2002: mock theta functions are the holomorphic parts of harmonic weak Maass forms. The "shadow" Ramanujan had intuited is a non-holomorphic correction term — real and precise, not mysterious.

But this resolution is extrinsic. It classifies mock theta functions by what they map to (harmonic Maass forms), not by what they are in themselves. The 算學 reading is more illuminating:

An ordinary theta function is an array whose coefficients, when you apply a modular transformation, map exactly onto another array in the same class. The transformation closes. A mock theta function is an array where the transformation almost closes — but leaves a systematic residue. That residue is itself a well-formed object. The 余项 (yú xiàng, remainder array) is not noise — it is structure.

This is not a metaphor for Zwegers' result. It is a constructive restatement of it — one that immediately suggests the right question: what is the algebra of remainder arrays? What operations on mock theta functions preserve the remainder structure? This is the missing Hecke theory.

V · The Order Problem — The Central Contribution

What "3rd order" actually means 阶的内在定义

This is the most precise place where 算學 offers something Western mathematics currently lacks. Ramanujan classified his mock theta functions as 3rd order, 5th order, and 7th order. Andrews and Hickerson confirmed: "the term 'order' must be regarded merely as a convenient label." There is no formal definition.

The Western approach attempts to define order via the weight of the associated Maass form, or via the level of the shadow. These are extrinsic — they classify mock theta functions by what they resemble from the outside, not by their internal structure.

The 算學 approach constructs an intrinsic definition:

The Constructive Definition of Order

Encode a mock theta function as an infinite coefficient array — q-series coefficients arranged in a grid, each row corresponding to a power of q. Define a set of permitted column operations on this array — the algorithmic rules analogous to Hecke-type operators, row shifts, weighted row additions.

An ordinary theta function is a fixed point of these operations: every permitted operation maps it onto another array in the same closed class. The operation closes completely.

A mock theta function is an array where the operations do not close — they produce a systematic deviation. But the deviation itself obeys a finite-depth recursion. The order is the minimum depth of this recursion: the minimum number of auxiliary arrays you must introduce to make the transformation system close.

A 3rd-order mock theta function requires 3 auxiliary arrays. A 5th-order requires 5. This number is not a label — it is a structural invariant of the array algebra.

Order as closure depth — click to step through
Each step = one auxiliary array introduced
The system closes when all deviation terms are accounted for
Depth of closure = order of the mock theta function
The key theorem this implies

If this constructive definition is correct, then "order" is no longer a convenient label — it becomes a theorem about the minimum closure dimension of an array system. A function that appears to be 7th order might actually be a disguised 3rd-order function when expressed in the right array basis. The apparent complexity reduces to a simpler recursion. This would explain why Ramanujan's original classification into exactly 3rd, 5th, and 7th orders is not arbitrary — these are the minimal closure dimensions permitted by the modular constraint structure.

VI · The Open Wounds — Precisely Stated

What remains. What 算學 can attempt. 待解之谜

Open · No Formal Definition
1. Intrinsic Definition of Mock Theta Order

Andrews and Hickerson (1991) explicitly state that "the term 'order'… must be regarded merely as a convenient label." Every Western attempt to define order is extrinsic — classifying by the associated Maass form weight, or by shadow level. No definition derives order from the internal structure of the mock theta function itself.

算學 proposal: Order = minimum number of auxiliary arrays required to close the transformation system. This is constructive, intrinsic, and produces a falsifiable prediction: a function that appears 7th-order in one basis should reduce to 3rd-order in the correct basis. The prediction can be tested computationally on Ramanujan's 17 examples.
Framework Missing · Partially Constructed
2. Hecke Theory for Mock Theta Functions

Hecke operators act on ordinary modular forms with beautiful algebraic regularity — they commute, they produce eigenforms, they generate the multiplicative structure of L-functions. No analogous operator theory exists for mock theta functions. We have the objects; we do not have their symmetry algebra. Dyson called for this in 1987. It is still missing.

算學 proposal: Define Hecke-type operators on the full array system — original array plus its auxiliary arrays plus the shadow map. The operator should commute with the closure recursion. Finding the correct operator definition on the augmented array system is the constructive path to the missing Hecke theory.
No Bijection Known · Natural Bridge
3. Rogers–Ramanujan Bijective Proof

The Rogers-Ramanujan identities equate two partition counts — partitions where parts differ by ≥ 2, and partitions into parts ≡ ±1 (mod 5). Both sides are proved equal analytically. But an explicit bijection — a one-to-one map between the two sets of partitions — does not exist. This is significant: an analytic proof confirms equality without showing which partition maps to which.

算學 proposal: A bijection is exactly a shù — an explicit procedure on the board. The natural search space: order-5 transformations on the partition lattice that permute residue classes while mapping one set to the other. The same framework used to seek crank-like witnesses for higher partition congruences.
Proved Analytically · Combinatorial Gap
4. Explicit Witnesses for p(pn + a) ≡ 0 mod p, p ≥ 13

Ono (2000) proved partition congruences exist for every prime p ≥ 5. But the combinatorial witness — a statistic that partitions the residue classes equally, as rank does for p=5,7 and crank does for p=11 — is unknown for p≥13. The proofs are existential; the witnesses are not constructed.

算學 proposal: Define an order-p transformation on the rod board. Seek the fixed-point structure. The witness is the labelling function generated by the cyclic orbit of the transformation. This is algorithmic search in a well-defined space of board operations — exactly what a 算學-trained AI system is built to explore.
VII · The Transformation Engine

What the encounter produces 变换引擎

The Ramanujan Machine (Technion, 2021) proved that algorithms can discover new continued fraction representations of fundamental constants matching Ramanujan's in elegance — and some remain unproven. The Ramanujan Library maps formulas as nodes in a hypergraph, discovering connections by numerical matching.

These are already operating in a proto-算學 mode: they don't derive from axioms — they search the space of transformations and verify numerical consistency.

But a fully realised 算學 AI would go further. It would not search for formulas — it would manipulate arrays directly on a virtual rod board, learning permitted operations through reinforcement, discovering invariants through pattern compression, and explaining its findings as a dissection sequence — not an attention heatmap but a transformation trace: what the array was, what operation was applied, what it became.

Applied to mock theta functions, it would not ask "what Maass form does this correspond to?" It would ask: "what sequence of elementary array operations, applied to this coefficient grid, reveals the invariant structure?" The answer is the shù. The invariant is the 理. The closure depth is the order.

This is the precise technical contribution: not proof assistance, but a new instrument for perceiving structure — one that makes truth visible through operation rather than deriving it from priors.

Closing · The Scene

What the encounter looks like

Imagine a scholar at a desk — not chalk and blackboard, but a luminous grid. A digital rod board extending into higher dimensions. Ramanujan's 17 mock theta functions are spread out as flickering coefficient arrays.

She applies a transformation — a specific column shift. A shadow array appears beside the original: the 余项 made visible, the failure to close rendered as a concrete object rather than a philosophical embarrassment. She applies another operation. The shadow transforms predictably. A third. The system closes: the original array, the auxiliary array, and the transformation rule form a complete, self-sufficient algebra.

She has just discovered that a function Ramanujan labelled 7th order is, in the correct array basis, a disguised 3rd-order function. The extra complexity was a choice of basis, not a feature of the structure. Order is not a label — it is a theorem about minimum closure dimension.

Ramanujan wrote in his final letter that he had found "the first few steps" of a path. He died before he could go further. What he lacked was not genius. He lacked a tradition — a systematic discipline built around the epistemology he was already naturally operating from.

That tradition existed. It was codified in rod-notation in 13th-century Hangzhou. It asked: what transformation produces this result? It proved by showing the dissection. It defined numbers as procedures. It treated the method as the primary object and the proof as its trace.

The two traditions never met. Ramanujan arrived at Cambridge in 1914 and was given Hardy — a magnificent collaborator, but one who lived on the other side of the epistemological divide. He was given formal proofs for the results he had already seen. What nobody gave him was the language for how he saw them.

算學 is that language. The open problems are the scar tissue of the non-encounter. And the transformation engine — a machine that does not verify truth but makes it visible through operation — is what finally builds the bridge.
